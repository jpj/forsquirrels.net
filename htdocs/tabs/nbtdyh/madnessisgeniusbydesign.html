All strings tune down one half step, but tune the G string (or the 3rd) up 
to Bb.  From low to high, the guitar should be at bE, bA, bD, bB, bB, bE.  
Make sure your second and third strings are unison (or they ring the same 
note, creating a sound resemblant to a 12-string guitar).

__ denotes a tie between notes
/ or \ are up and down slides respectively
. is a palm mute given to a single note or chord
~~ is a vibrato
