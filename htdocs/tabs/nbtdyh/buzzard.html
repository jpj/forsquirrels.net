Standard Tuning.  (ha!)

Well, like the rest of the album, the song is one-half step down.

The intro is played with harmonics, running sixteenth notes, but 
be careful.  The ninth fret harmonics are intended to sound a 
perfect third above the open string  (these can also be played 
at the fourth fret, but i found the sound more resonant at the 
ninth.).  Being off the fret the slightest quarter of an inch 
will give you a different pitch (listen to Alice In Chains "
Again" at the breakdown (doot-doot) part, both of those two 
different harmonics are near the third fret.). Well, here goes.
